Homemade Bread

Description

a simple, comforting food made from scratch using basic kitchen staples like flour, water, salt, and yeast. Unlike store-bought bread, it is free from preservatives, resulting in a fresher, better-tasting loaf with a soft, fluffy, or chewy texture.a simple, comforting food made from scratch using basic kitchen staples like flour, water, salt, and yeast. Unlike store-bought bread, it is free from preservatives, resulting in a fresher, better-tasting loaf with a soft, fluffy, or chewy texture.

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup warm water
  • 1 ½ (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
  • ⅛ cup bread flour
  • ½ tablespoon white sugar½ tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 cup quick cooking oats
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 ¼ cups warm water
  • ¾ tablespoon salt
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 5 cups bread flour

Step:

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar in a large bowl. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add oats, whole wheat flour, and vegetable oil to the yeast mixture. Stir well.
  3. Gradually add bread flour and salt to the mixture. Knead until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover with a towel, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and shape it into a loaf. Place in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Let the dough rise again for 30-45 minutes until nearly doubled.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
